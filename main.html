What is Hackathon?

A hackathon is a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, domain experts, and others collaborate intensively on software projects.

Why Hackathon?

It is a great opportunity to show your skills and learn new things. You can also meet new people and make new friends.

How to join?

Just click the register button and fill the form. You will receive an email with further instructions.
